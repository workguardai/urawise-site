Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Urawise ("we", "us", or "our") is a digital marketing and Google Ads consultancy based in Madrid, Spain. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

When you use the free AI tools on our website, we process the information you enter (such as your business type, website URL, or advertising goals) to generate personalised recommendations. This information is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate responses and is not stored permanently by us.

When you contact us via WhatsApp, we may store the conversation history temporarily (up to 24 hours) to provide context-aware responses. We do not share this information with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to deliver the services you have requested — generating Google Ads recommendations, website audits, SEO analysis, and responding to your enquiries. We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services: Anthropic Claude API (AI responses), Google PageSpeed Insights API (website performance data), Google Search Console API (with your explicit authorisation), and Cloudflare Workers (request processing). Each of these services has its own privacy policy.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We do not use analytics tools that identify individual users.

Data Retention

Information entered into our free tools is processed in real time and not stored after your session ends. WhatsApp conversation history is stored for a maximum of 24 hours to enable contextual replies, after which it is automatically deleted.

Your Rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at org@greenhelsinki.com.
